Hindenburg bookmarklet ⫷

Scout locations more easily.

Switch between Google Maps, Bing Maps + Nokia Here with one click.

Google, Bing and Nokia each have their own collections of aerial imagery. This bookmarklet makes it easy to switches from one to the next, maintaining your geolocation.

To Install

Drag this link: H⫷ to your bookmarks bar... Click it when you are on Google Maps, Bing Maps or here.com.
